Sample Architecture

How do we achieve such cutting edge results? ML architecture is key of course! Here at Shallow Mind we move away from traditional industry results based approaches. We also neglect simplicity and interpretability in our architecture. Rather above all we prioritize aesthetic quality of architecture diagrams. Do we get good results and do our architectures make sense? Nope, but algorithm output doesn’t matter in the slightest. After all, since war is a 100% procedural card game which does not allow players to make a single decision it’s impossible to lose with more than a 50% probability :)
